Darel Global Energy Service is a Private Limited Company with the aim of carrying out business in Engineering works and Procurement with specialization in the Engineering and Corporate Energy Services. Darel Global Energy Services is proud of its expertise, efficiency and technical skill in Engineering Operations and Procurement. Due to strict adherence to safety regulations, the company has maintained an enviable position in the Engineering Section. The Company was incorporated with the primary objective of helping to develop and support the world quest for energy solution due to the increasing demand in the Engineering, Procurement, Instrumentation and Facilities Maintenance in Nigeria Energy sector of our economy.
